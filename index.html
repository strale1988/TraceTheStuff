TraceTheStuff is a small suite of independent tracing and diagnostic tools built by StrApps. Each app focuses on one job: TraceTheBreak maps structural and infrastructure faults, and TraceTheToxin tracks contamination and hazard dispersion. TraceMyWay and TraceMyBike, for journey and cycling tracking, are on the way.
